Clustering Analysis

In order to better understand how our K-means model categorizes tracks and their attributes, Tableau was used to vizualize important characteristics of each cluster. By using the dropdown, all 9 clusters can be selected and assessed in two dimensions simultaneously. On the left, a TreeMap is used to display the hierarchal arrangement of attribute averages in a given cluster. On the right, a bubble chart is used to exhibit the top 10 appearing genres in that cluster.
